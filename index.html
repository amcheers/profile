About

Unsatisfied with my previous career, I began to research new skills and opportunities that would challenge my mind and provide me with an environment where potential for growth and learning would be an integral part of the culture. Once I landed upon coding as a possible avenue, I looked up in demand languages, bought a new laptop, the Python Crash Course book and got to work. I fell in love immediately. After 8 months of self study, I found a Web Development bootcamp in Amsterdam to further my skills and push me toward the next step of job readiness in the field. There I furthered my understanding of basic programming principles, expanded my toolbox of technologies through an introduction to and use of API's, databases like PostgreSQL, and Bootstrap to name a few. I learned team collaboration through Git and Github, and completed and deployed multiple web applications to production using Heroku.
